# American Liberty Order - Website Context

## Organization Overview
The American Liberty Order (ALO) is a comprehensive civic organization dedicated to defending constitutional principles, expanding citizen influence, and strengthening American liberty through concrete policy reforms and community engagement.

## Core Mission & Values
- **Mission**: Defend constitutional principles, expand citizen influence, and strengthen American liberty through implementable reforms that reduce government overreach, curb cronyism, protect individual rights, and empower communities
- **Core Values**: Limited government, individual liberty, constitutional fidelity, local control, rule of law, transparency, accountability, and market-based solutions
- **Philosophy**: Skeptical of centralized power and entrenched bureaucracies; emphasizes practical governance and citizen sovereignty

## Key Policy Pillars

### 1. Economic Freedom & Anti-Cronyism
Combat regulatory capture and remove government barriers protecting established interests. Focus on Medicare bid reform, occupational licensing reform, Right to Repair, Civil Asset Forfeiture reform, and healthcare price transparency.

### 2. Food & Health Sovereignty
Freedom to choose food and health products, prioritizing local production and informed consent. Includes raw dairy access, cottage-food expansion, medical freedom protections.

### 3. Property Rights
Defend ownership against government and private overreach through HOA reform, Eminent Domain reform, and Mineral rights protection.

### 4. Government Accountability & Reform
Congressional stock-trading bans, term limits, "End Lobbying: The Citizen Voice Portal", Regulatory State Reform (REINS Act), Legislative Reform, Emergency Powers Reform.

### 5. Election Integrity
Easy to vote, hard to cheat. Mandatory voter ID, paper ballots with audit trails, chain-of-custody protocols, timely results with risk-limiting audits.

### 6. Constitutional Rights Suite
- **Free Speech**: Protect against platform censorship, anti-SLAPP measures
- **Second Amendment**: Individual gun rights, self-defense, constitutional carry, national reciprocity
- **Parental Rights & Education**: Curriculum transparency, medical treatment consent, homeschooling protections
- **Individual Liberty & Conscience**: Protect negative liberties and freedom of conscience
- **Reproductive Rights**: First-trimester access with robust adoption infrastructure

### 7. Foreign Policy & National Interest
America-first, security-focused, fiscally prudent. Accountability in foreign aid, merit-based immigration reform, defense-centric posture, burden-sharing with allies.

### 8. Monetary & Currency Reform
Increase Fed transparency, oppose CBDCs with privacy protections, explore sound-money systems, promote currency competition and banking reform.

### 9. Tax Reform
Avoid taxing labor; discourage dynastic wealth. Eliminate income tax and replace with consumption-based alternatives, wealth/inheritance taxes with thresholds.

### 10. Drug Policy Reform
End the war on drugs with regulated, harm-reduction orientation; licensed sellers with comprehensive support services.

### 11. Housing Reform
Curb financialization and institutional ownership; increase housing supply. End restrictive zoning, cap corporate ownership, limit foreign ownership.

## Strategic Approach
- **State-First Strategy**: Begin with state-level reforms to build credibility, then scale to federal level
- **Phased Implementation**: Prioritize achievable wins before ambitious reforms
- **Coalition Building**: Appeal across conservatives, progressives, and rural constituencies
- **Transparency & Accountability**: Independent enforcement, public dashboards, sunset provisions
- **Education Before Advocacy**: Emphasize understanding before political action
